Common WASHING MACHINE
Problems We Fix

Our factory-trained technicians can fix all types of washing machine problems. Here are some of the most common washing machine repair services we encounter.
The washing machine doesn't run
Your machine may not start for many reasons, from simple ones such as power connections to more complicated ones like broken controls. Check your water supply to ensure that valves are open and nothing is blocking the valve screens. A broken water supply system will also cause your machine not to run. Our highly qualified washer repair team will do everything to get your washer working again as soon as possible.
The washer is vibrating
Placing your washer on an uneven floor will cause it to vibrate when in operation. An unbalanced load may also cause the machine to vibrate even on all surfaces. Carefully level and lock your washer's feet on an uneven floor for the best results. Carefully level and lock your washer's feet on an uneven floor for the best results.
Washer is leaking
If your washer is leaking, you do not have to call washer repair services immediately; it could be as a result of any of the following:
-The washer is not standard.
-Water supply pipes are not attached properly
-Clogged washer drains
The washer is making strange noises
Foreign objects caught in the washer could cause strange sounds like gurgling, clicking, or humming. Clogged drains and internal sensors may also cause noises.
Washer won't spin or drain
Improper loading may cause your machine not to spin or spin with difficulty, while using too much detergent may clog the drains. Let our washer repair professionals help you diagnose and repair the washing machine to avoid costly issues in the future. Let our washer repair professionals help you diagnose and repair the washing machine to avoid costly issues in the future.
Washing machine stops mid-cycle
If your washer starts to spin but stops mid cycle, it could be due to:
-Defective lid-switch assembly
-Broken control board
-Defective timer
-Improper loading If these are not the cause, you need to contact your washer repair technician to inspect your machine further.

What does the washing machine repair service Include?
Washing machine repair services include a complete diagnosis, repair, and part replacement required to get your machine up and running again. Our technician will also answer your questions and give maintenance tips and advice.
[02]
How much does it cost to repair a washer?
It will cost you an average of $200 to get your washer running again. While replacing a drain pipe costs around $120, the washer gear case replacement may cost over $300. Replacing the water level sensor, one of the most frequent washer repairs, will cost you about $150.
[03]
What causes some washer repairs to be expensive?
The cost of washer repair depends on several factors, like the maintenance history, the number of parts replaced, the type of parts replaced, and so on. For example, replacing the washer gearcase is more expensive than replacing the water level sensor.
[04]
Is it worth having a washing machine repaired?
Yes and No. Some parts of the washer, such as the water level sensor, fail more often than others. They are also relatively cheap and easy to replace to get your washer running again. An excellent way to determine whether it is worth repairing your washer is to consider the repair cost and the age of the washer.
If your washer is less than five years old and the repair cost is less than half its price, it may be economical to repair as it still has years of service in it. On the other hand, if your machine is more than ten years old, buying a new model with current technology and better energy efficiency makes more sense. Call our washing machine repair team to diagnose what you exactly need.
[05]
How long does a washer repair take?
Typically, it takes our washer repair technicians a few hours to repair your washer. Replacing simple parts may only take an hour or so for well-maintained washers. For washers without proper servicing for a long time, it may take up to a day to repair and replace worn-out parts. We advise that you perform regular maintenance and repairs on your washing machine to keep it in good working condition.
